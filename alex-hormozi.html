Alex Hormozi

Alex Hormozi is a renowned entrepreneur, author, investor, and philanthropist best known for scaling businesses and teaching others how to build sustainable, profitable enterprises. Born in 1989, Hormozi has made a name for himself in the fitness and business education industries. He is the founder of Acquisition.com, a company that invests in and advises growing businesses.

Hormozi first made waves in the fitness world, building a chain of highly successful gyms and eventually transitioning into helping other gym owners increase their revenue through his company Gym Launch. Through this, he demonstrated a repeatable system for sales, lead generation, and operational excellence.

In 2021, Hormozi sold several of his companies and shifted his focus toward investing in other founders through Acquisition.com. He frequently shares in-depth, transparent insights into his journey via social media and YouTube, where his following has rapidly grown. His no-fluff style and generous value delivery have earned him respect in the entrepreneurial space.

Hormozi is also the author of several best-selling business books, including "$100M Offers" and "$100M Leads," which have been widely adopted by business owners worldwide. These books focus on creating irresistible offers and generating massive demand, respectively.

Unlike many business influencers, Hormozi is known for his data-driven approach and focus on discipline, not hype. He avoids flashy lifestyles and instead champions efficiency, long-term thinking, and high-leverage actions. His wife, Leila Hormozi, is also a key business partner in their ventures.

Alex continues to invest in entrepreneurs aiming to scale their businesses from 3 to 50 million dollars in revenue. His impact is especially felt among young business builders, who often regard him as a modern-day strategist in the online and offline business worlds.

For more, visit acquisition.com or check his educational content on YouTube.
